Teleprompter for Video Support

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026

Teleprompter for Video is designed to keep your scripts private.

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal data for the developer.

The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or a developer-operated server.

Scripts And iCloud

Scripts you create are stored on your device. If iCloud is enabled for the app, scripts may also sync through your private iCloud account using Apple's iCloud services.

The developer does not receive, read, sell, or share your scripts.

Apple Services

iCloud sync is provided by Apple and depends on the Apple Account signed in on your device. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's privacy terms and settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Teleprompter for Video support page.
