iCloud Sync

Teleprompter for Video uses the Apple Account already signed in on your device. There is no separate login inside the app.

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Sign in with an Apple Account.
  3. Enable iCloud Drive.
  4. Make sure iCloud is enabled for Teleprompter for Video.
  5. Reopen Teleprompter for Video and tap Refresh iCloud.

If iCloud is unavailable, scripts continue to save locally on the device.

Prompting

  • Use Play or Pause to control auto-scroll.
  • Use speed controls to tune reading pace.
  • Use horizontal mirror for beam-splitter teleprompter rigs.
  • Use vertical mirror for inverted mounts.
  • Tap the prompt area in full-screen mode to show or hide controls.
